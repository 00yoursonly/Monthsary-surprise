The Story of Us

We first became classmates during our 1st year in college, but at that time we barely talked to each other. We were both freshmen, still adjusting to everything, and we didn’t really notice each other. And when our second semester started, we finally had small interactions because we were always grouped together. But honestly, I still didn’t pay much attention to her coz I only knew her by her last name, nothing more.

Things started to change in our 2nd year. That’s when we slowly became closer. We talked more, but still casually, mostly about school. I was even surprised when she made the first move in chat. She would ask me if I was going to class, if we had a professor, or other simple school related questions. It wasn’t anything deep, but it made me notice her more.

During finals, when we had our cultural show, and we practiced outside. And that’s where we really started getting close. One time after practice, a classmate invited us to ride home together. We ended up going with her, but I was riding with my friend while she rode with the guy who liked her. That moment, I felt a small sting of jealousy, and that’s when I realized I had a crush on her.

Another time, we rode home again as a group, but this time she rode with me because my friend rode with her boyfriend. I remember feeling so happy. After that, I found myself mentioning her name all the time. Even my friends noticed. Eventually, we became a circle, an unexpected friendship that made us even closer.

Then one day, I couldn’t hold it in anymore. I confessed. She rejected me kindly, saying she was straight and only saw me as a friend. I was scared she would avoid me, even though I made it clear I wasn’t asking her to return my feelings. After that, I became shy around her, but she didn’t pull away. In fact, we became even closer. She rejected me a few more times but I was so persistent.

As time passed, I noticed she was slowly letting me in. I didn’t assume anything, because to me she was out of my league. But she started becoming clingy, and eventually our friends found out that I like her. I caught myself pursuing her without even planning to, I started visiting her house late at night just to give her favorite snacks, staying there until dawn, tracking her period so I’d know when to be extra gentle with her, giving her flowers whenever we met. Everything I did came naturally.

After months of pursuing her, she finally allowed me to court her. And after a few more months, she said yes. She told me she fell for my consistency, and in that moment, I felt like the luckiest woman alive. Even now, months into our relationship, I still ask her if we’re really together, if she’s really my girlfriend, because sometimes I still can’t believe she’s already mine. She’s the person I now plan my future with, the person I lean on through everything. She’s different coz loving her taught me how to love myself too.

Our relationship isn’t perfect. We had arguments, we had misunderstandings, and we’re still learning how to handle things together. But I always tell her that it’s normal coz we’re still learning each other. I will never get tired of understanding her. Our misunderstandings never make me see her any less and she’s the only woman who has ever made me feel this way.

And so, the chapters write themselves...
